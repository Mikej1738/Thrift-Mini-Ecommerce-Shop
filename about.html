Our story

Gataka Threads was born in Nkaimurunya, Kajiado—where heritage meets modern design. We craft everyday clothing that honors local culture while delivering comfort and durability.

We partner with regional artisans and suppliers, prioritizing ethical production and fair pricing. Every piece is designed to be worn, loved, and lived in.

What we value

  • Quality: Fabrics that last, cuts that fit.
  • Culture: Designs inspired by local heritage.
  • Community: Sourcing and collaboration within Kajiado.
